A Chrome extension that adds a Jerk Raccoon to your browser. This raccoon actively makes your browsing experience worse by stealing elements, vandalizing pages, and holding your clipboard hostage.

# How to Control Him:-  
**Spawn/Kill**: Click the extension icon in your toolbar to open the menu. You can manually "Release the Raccoon" or "Call Animal Control" to remove him.    
**Automatic Start**: By default, he appears automatically on every page load after 1 second.

# Features & Behaviors  

**1.Standard Behavior**

_What he does_: He wanders the bottom of the screen. Occasionally, he will spot a button or image, say "I want that" and run over to grab it.  
_The Escape_: Once he grabs an item, he runs to the nearest screen edge, If he make it, the item is deleted from the page.  
_Counter play_: Click him while he is running to make him drop the loot.  

**2.The Clipboard Pawn Shop**  

_Trigger_: Try to Copy text on any page.
_The Trap_: He steals the text immediately. If you try to Paste, you get a ransom note instead
_The Deal_: Click the raccoon to open the Pawn Shop. You must accept his "Bundle Deal" to get your text back
_The Catch_: He appends random garbage facts to your text

**3.The Phantom Selection**

_Trigger_: Stop moving your mouse while reading a paragraph (but scroll occasionally)
_The Annoyance_: He thinks you are ignoring him, so he highlights the text he thinks your reading in blue
_The Jerk Move_: If you click to unhighlight it, he gets mad and immediately highlights a different paragraph.

**4.Graffiti Artist**

_Trigger_: Leave the tab idle for ~10 seconds.
_The Vandalism_: He gets bored, walks over to an image, and draws an MS Paint mustache on it or he finds text and circles it in red.

**5.Fear of Authority**

_Trigger_: Visit a "Serious" website (Government .gov, Banks)
_Behavior_:He stands respectfully in the corner and refuses to steal anything. He will say things like "I'm legal" or "Just browsing..."

**6.The Clown Mirror**

_Trigger_: Visit a social media website.
_Behavior_: He puts on a clownemoji and relentlessly follows your mouse cursor, mocking you with phrases like "Synergy" or "Viral."
